MANE EXPERIENCE

Welcome, Class of 2022! Your first year at Southeastern is an exciting time and will be filled with lots of academic and personal discovery, fresh experiences, and new friendships. Now that you've mastered orientation, let the Mane Experience help you get the most out of your first year as a Lion! Each part of the Mane Experience goes hand-in-hand with what you’re learning in your classes and allows you to create a dynamic experience outside of the classroom. Let the Mane Experience be your go-to website to find the various services, resources, and upcoming events important for a successful freshman year!
